# Manager, Content Partnerships

**Department:** Content Partnerships
**Location:** New York, NY (Hybrid)
**Reports to:** Director, Content Partnerships
**Openings:** 2

## About EverPass

EverPass Media is a comprehensive media platform dedicated to commercial businesses, that aggregates, distributes and enables streaming of live sports and entertainment content, and offers a wide array of consumer engagement and performance marketing tools for bars, restaurants, hotels and other commercial venues. Launched initially as the exclusive distributor of NFL Sunday Ticket to commercial establishments in the United States, EverPass partners with rightsholders, distribution partners and business owners to unlock greater access to premium live events and drive business growth. EverPass was founded in 2023 in partnership with RedBird Capital Partners and 32 Equity, the strategic investment arm of the National Football League. TKO Group Holdings, parent company of UFC and WWE, joined as an investor in 2024.

## About the Role

Managers are the operational and strategic backbone of Content Partnerships. We are hiring two into the same team, from the same talent pool. Each Manager carries a set of workstreams end to end: translating between what is technically possible and what is commercially strategic, keeping deals moving from signed through activation, and running the cross-functional communication the partnership book depends on.

This role suits a media-savvy operator who is equally comfortable working with Engineering and Product on APIs and scheduling, and building the strategy, analysis and communication that keep leagues, streamers and internal stakeholders aligned. The two hires are expected to bring complementary strengths, one weighted toward content operations and product, one toward strategy and communication, while sharing the same core profile.

## What You'll Do

**Content operations and product**

- Own content API and backend scheduler workstreams end to end, coordinating with Engineering and Operations to onboard new content and properties.
- Bridge what is technically possible in product and engineering with what is realistic and strategic for content onboarding, translating technical constraints into commercial decisions and back again.
- Manage cross-functional workstreams for live deals from signed through activation, tracking readiness, deliverables and gates across a 60+ partner book supported by existing operating systems.

**Strategy, growth and communication**

- Refill the top of the funnel: source, research and qualify new content partnership opportunities across sports, media and technology.
- Run market research, competitor benchmarking and landscape mapping to inform sequencing and strategy.
- Own framing, go-to-market and analytics for partnership initiatives, and produce the decks, one-pagers and reporting that keep every stakeholder on one read.
- Carry the cross-functional communication across Finance, Marketing and Sales, keeping workstreams synced and escalations moving.

## What You'll Bring

- 4 to 7 years in content partnerships, business development, program or product management, strategy, or operations within sports, media, streaming or digital content.
- Comfort operating at the intersection of technical and commercial work: you can partner with Engineering and Product on APIs, scheduling and onboarding, and turn that into partner-facing strategy.
- Strong project management: you own multiple workstreams end to end without dropping deliverables or reporting obligations.
- Excellent written and verbal communication. You can build the deck, run the analysis and keep cross-functional partners aligned on one read.
- Comfort with ambiguity and a fast-paced, growth-focused environment.
- Bonus: familiarity with out-of-home or out-of-market sports rights, content licensing, or venue and commercial distribution.
