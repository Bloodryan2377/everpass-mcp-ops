# Director, Content Partnerships

**Department:** Content Partnerships
**Location:** New York, NY (Hybrid)
**Reports to:** Chief Content Officer

## About EverPass

EverPass Media is a comprehensive media platform dedicated to commercial businesses, that aggregates, distributes and enables streaming of live sports and entertainment content, and offers a wide array of consumer engagement and performance marketing tools for bars, restaurants, hotels and other commercial venues. Launched initially as the exclusive distributor of NFL Sunday Ticket to commercial establishments in the United States, EverPass partners with rightsholders, distribution partners and business owners to unlock greater access to premium live events and drive business growth. EverPass was founded in 2023 in partnership with RedBird Capital Partners and 32 Equity, the strategic investment arm of the National Football League. TKO Group Holdings, parent company of UFC and WWE, joined as an investor in 2024.

## About the Role

Content Partnerships owns the out-of-home rights that let leagues, streamers and distributors reach commercial venues without cannibalizing their direct-to-consumer base. That book now runs across 40+ live deals, from origination through signed, plus the renewals, amendments and extensions around them.

As Director, Content Partnerships, you own that book. You review deals independently, sequence them for maximum commercial value, own the counterparty relationships, and carry cross-functional escalation across Acquisition, Operations, Strategy, Legal, Finance and Marketing. This is a high-visibility, high-ownership role for a senior operator with deep sports-media experience, strong deal instincts and a bias for action. You will lead a small team of Managers who carry the operational and strategic workstreams.

## What You'll Do

- Own the full lifecycle of EverPass's content partnership book, from origination through negotiation, papering, renewal and expansion.
- Independently review deal drafts, term sheets and contracts before they advance, without relying on Finance or an adjacent function for content-side review.
- Sequence the book: decide which deals get papered first, in what order, for maximum leverage across leagues, networks, streamers and distributors.
- Drive the deals in active negotiation to close, currently the largest stage of the pipeline, while keeping origination and top-of-funnel moving.
- Own counterparty relationships with rights holders, leagues, platforms and distribution partners, and serve as the point of escalation across every function touching a deal.
- Partner with Legal on out-of-home (OOH) and out-of-market (OOM) rights structures that give commercial venues access without cannibalizing rights holders' direct-to-consumer base.
- Translate between commercial strategy, product and engineering realities, and legal structure so the whole function moves on one read.
- Manage, coach and set priorities for a team of Managers carrying operational and strategic workstreams.

## What You'll Bring

- 8+ years in content acquisition, distribution, partnerships or business development across sports, media or streaming, including direct ownership of deal execution.
- A track record structuring and closing licensing, distribution and OOH/OOM rights agreements with leagues, networks, streamers, MVPDs or vMVPDs.
- Strong deal instincts: you can read a term sheet, sequence a book of competing priorities and own negotiations end to end.
- A cross-functional operator's range: you move comfortably between commercial strategy, product and engineering constraints, and legal structure.
- Experience managing or mentoring a team, or clear readiness to step into people leadership.
- Comfort as the accountable owner when a deal stalls, and the judgment to know which relationships to protect and which terms to hold.
- Excellent written and verbal communication, with the executive presence to represent EverPass to senior counterparties.
